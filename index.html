All about GRIP

The Graduate Rotational Internship Program is an unique offer for students and recent graduates to experience and join The Sparks Foundation. In addition to skills specific tasks, we encourage interns to build a credible professional profile.

Completion Certificate

Completion Certificate is provided after success completion of one month of internship.


Opportunity to join TSF

Opportunity to be a part of TSF is given to best candidates and they are hired on special hiring plan.


Networking Opportunity

Networking opportunity as a global, multicultural and rare diverse working environment is available for interns.

Recommendation Letter

Recommendation Letter provided to top performing interns based on various assessment parameters.


Workshops/ guidance

Workshop and audience for personality insights, profile building, interviews skills and scrum training sessions.


Special Badges

Badges will be given to interns who have submitted tasks efficiently and helped the fellow interns in their tasks.
